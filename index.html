Welcome to compare this & that

This website was made to compare two ingredients you have found online to find the difference in them

Instead of having to manually go through the long list in each product to find a difference when shopping, Copy&Paste the ingredients you have and type in the seperation for each ingredient

And you should find the odd ones out! (+ingredients that are similar)

Made because I didn't want to go through the hassle of checking ingredients in each bottle to see what would/wouldn't work for skin
